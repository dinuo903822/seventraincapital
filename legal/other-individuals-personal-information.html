What personal information do we collect and use?

When you interact with us, for example by visiting our offices, corresponding with us by email, post or telephone, or speaking to us at an event or meeting, we collect personal information about you. Where we collect your sensitive personal information (which, depending on the requirements set out in relevant applicable data protection law, may include your passwords, financial information, biometric information, sexual orientation, medical records and history or information about physical, physiological and mental health conditions), we will process it in accordance with the requirements of applicable data protection law (including obtaining express written consent, where required).

When you visit our offices, we may process the following personal information about you:

  • your name, email address and employer,
  • your host at SoftBank Investment Advisers,
  • CCTV footage of you in our offices,
  • the time and date of your visit(s), and
  • details about any non-disclosure agreements you have signed.

We need this information in order to give you access to our offices. If you do not provide it, you may not be permitted to access our offices.

If you are in our offices and log on to our guest WiFi, we will process your IP address and device details. Further details about the use of our guest WiFi may be found in the Guest WiFi Terms of Use.

If you send us an email, we will collect any personal information included in your correspondence with us, for example, your name and contact details.

If you give us your business card or other contact details, we may process personal information such as your:

  • name,
  • job title,
  • employer and professional history,
  • contact details, and
  • other details available on websites used for social media or networking purposes, such as LinkedIn.

This information may also be provided to us by your employer where we interact with them as part of our investment activities.

For what purposes do we process personal information?

We process this personal information in our legitimate interests, which include:

  • ensuring our investor and business contact records are up-to-date,
  • for the management of our business,
  • identifying opportunities to develop our business,
  • providing a guest WiFi network for visitors to our offices,
  • in accordance with our internal compliance policies,
  • ensuring our premises and confidential information are kept secure, and
  • keeping a log of visitors to our offices.

We may also be required to process your personal information in accordance with our legal and regulatory obligations. If we are required to process your sensitive personal information, we will process it in accordance with applicable data protection law (including obtaining express written consent, where required).

We may provide you with information about our products and services and other information which we think you may find interesting. We may send you such information by post, e-mail and/or telephone, unless you have asked us not to do so.

We will not provide your personal data to external organisations for their own marketing purposes unless we seek your explicit consent before doing so.
